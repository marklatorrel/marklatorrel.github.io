This is Arkabia

The reason I'm studing Full Stack Web Development, is for me to be able to understand programming concepts in order to manage developers. I'm creating a prototype in Figma of a webpage called Arkabia.
Arkabia's goal is to make foreign trade available for everyone by creating a platform that brings together all the actors who participate in the process, let it be importation or exportation, and make the process fluid and intuitive among them. Imagine a webpage that tells you step by step what you have to do in order to bring a product from another country to yours, minimizing the risk in every corner.
